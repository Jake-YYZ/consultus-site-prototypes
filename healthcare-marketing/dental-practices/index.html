Common Questions

Dental Practices marketing, answered.

Can you integrate with our PMS for closed-loop reporting?

We support Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, and most major systems via Zapier or direct integration. Closed-loop reporting is the only way we measure success.

Do you handle multi-location groups?

Yes. Multi-location is where most generalist agencies fail, because the SEO and ad architecture has to be different. We've specific playbooks for 2 to 25 location groups.

What about practices with high insurance dependency?

We tune messaging for insurance-first audiences and run separate creative for fee-for-service or membership-plan models. Both work, with the right architecture.
