06 / Scope of Work

What you're getting, exactly.

Deliverables, client responsibilities, and exclusions for each service included in this engagement.

01 / Website Development

Custom WordPress build, performance-optimized.

Consultus Digital will design and develop a custom, performance-optimized website for [Client Name], built on WordPress and aligned with [Client Name]'s brand guidelines.

Deliverables

  • Discovery workshop and sitemap approval
  • Custom WordPress design, up to 30 pages
  • Mobile-responsive build (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • On-page SEO foundation: meta, schema, sitemap, robots
  • Integration with CRM, forms, and call tracking
  • Two rounds of design revisions
  • Launch QA, redirects, and 30-day post-launch support

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide brand assets, logos, and photography
  • Provide access to domain, hosting, and CMS
  • Approve designs and revisions promptly

Exclusions

  • Ongoing maintenance beyond 30-day post-launch window
  • Additional pages beyond the 30-page scope (quoted separately)
  • E-commerce functionality
  • Custom plugin or integration development beyond listed integrations
02 / Landing Page Development

One conversion-focused page, built for paid traffic.

Consultus Digital will design and develop a custom, conversion-focused landing page optimized for paid traffic, lead capture, and performance tracking.

Deliverables

  • Conversion-focused layout, page structure, and ad copy
  • Custom design, two rounds of revisions
  • Mobile-responsive WordPress build
  • Lead capture forms routed to CRM or email
  • Tracking verification for forms and key conversions

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide final copy, offers, disclaimers, brand assets
  • Approve designs and revisions promptly
  • Provide CRM or email integration details

Exclusions

  • Multi-variant CRO testing (covered under Ongoing CRO)
  • Additional landing pages beyond the one included

Creative engineered to drive action.

Ongoing Monthly Refresh

  • Creative reporting: CTR, CPA, fatigue signals

Exclusions
