The Challenge

Where Tonic Eye Care & Vision Therapy started.

Context

Vision therapy is a niche specialty most patients do not know exists. The clinic had a strong clinical reputation locally but a digital surface that read like every other optometrist. Paid media efforts had defaulted to generic eye-exam offers that did not differentiate the practice.

The Approach

What we actually did.

01.

Produced UGC creative with parents and patients walking through what vision therapy actually does. Specific, story-driven, and category-specific.

02.

Restructured the local SEO around vision therapy as a category, not as a sub-feature of an optometry practice.

03.

Layered Meta retargeting against the specific traffic that was researching specialty topics on the new landing pages.

The Outcome

What moved.

Result

Engagement on the specialty-focused UGC ads lifted 94% over the previous baseline. Patient inquiries shifted from generic eye-exam questions to qualified vision-therapy consultations. The acquisition flywheel is now repeatable, not bespoke per quarter.
